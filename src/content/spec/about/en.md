# About

Hi, I'm **Dr. Charon**.

I'm a Systems & Network Administrator based in Montreal, Canada. Most of my day revolves around:

- Windows Server
- Active Directory
- Microsoft 365
- Microsoft Entra ID
- Intune
- VMware & Hyper-V
- Networking & Monitoring
- Automation & Troubleshooting

I started this blog to document problems from real production environments.

Most issues aren't caused by a single misconfiguration. More often, they're the result of old settings, forgotten changes, permissions, synchronization, networking, and a bit of bad timing.

Here you'll find:

- Real-world troubleshooting
- PowerShell and command-line examples
- Event logs and error messages
- Step-by-step investigations
- Lessons learned along the way
- Working solutions

This isn't meant to be a complete reference or another collection of tutorials. It's simply a place to document what actually happened, how I approached the problem, and what eventually fixed it.

**If a post helps someone avoid the same rabbit hole I went down, then this blog has done its job.**

## Beyond Work

Outside of IT, I'm also passionate about music.

Some of my free time goes into:

- Live sound
- PA systems
- Digital mixing consoles
- Recording
- Mixing & mastering

In the end, troubleshooting a network and troubleshooting an audio signal aren't all that different.

You follow the signal until you find where it disappears.
